About Me

I moved to Charlottesville Virginia in October of 2016. I earned my Bachelor's of Music in Vocal Perofrmance from The Boston Conservatory in 2014, and I'm currently working on a Master's in Arts and Culture Management from Rome Business School. Art, in any form, whether it be music, dance, theater, or painting, is the soul of the human race. It's what makes life beautiful. Modern technology innovation has made the human race connected in ways that have lead to the expansion and accessability of art that have made existance even more amazing. I want to bring art and technology together to continue to enrich lives with beautiful music, movies, pottery, and everything else that art brings to the table.
